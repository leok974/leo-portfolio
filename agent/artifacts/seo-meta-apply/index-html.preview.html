About me

I started as a 3D game designer because I loved building worlds. When AI surged, I was skeptical—honestly, a little afraid it would flatten creativity. Instead of resisting, I learned to use it as a tool. That shift put me on a path to ML/AI engineering where my 3D instincts—composition, motion, feel—guide how I design systems. Today I build human-centered AI for creative work—assistants, pipelines, multimedia tools—and I'm focused on pushing the edge where models meet art: interactive graphics, audio, and video. I'm here to turn ideas into things you can touch.
